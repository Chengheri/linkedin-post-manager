Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the LinkedIn Post Analyser ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our application.

Information Collection and Use

The LinkedIn Post Analyser is a client-side application that operates entirely within your web browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy in the following ways:

  • No Server Storage: All your data, including posts and settings, is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • LinkedIn API Access: When you connect your LinkedIn account, we request access to your posts, profile information, and publishing capabilities through LinkedIn's official API.
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth tokens provided by LinkedIn are stored securely in your browser's localStorage and are only used to make authorized API requests to LinkedIn's servers on your behalf.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not collect, share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

The following data may be stored locally in your browser:

  • LinkedIn authentication tokens
  • Your post content, schedules, and engagement metrics
  • Application preferences and settings

This data remains exclusively on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or by using the app's disconnect feature.

LinkedIn Data Access

This application requests the following permissions from LinkedIn:

  • r_emailaddress: To access your email address for account identification
  • r_liteprofile: To access basic profile information
  • w_member_social: To create, read, update, and delete your posts

All interactions with LinkedIn are governed by LinkedIn's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
